Source 2:
Home Appliance Show in Tennessee, 1949
This picture shows a home appliance show where new technologies for use in the home, such as stoves, refrigerators, and washing machines, were introduced to potential buyers. The purpose of home appliance shows was to make consumers aware of new technologies for the home, so they would be more likely to buy them.    
 
 

Source: Provided by the National Archives, this image is in the public domain and is available at:
https://www.docsteach.org/documents/ document/photograph-electric-appliance-show.
